wammocheck

A bookmarklet to reveal Walmart Ammo data

Instructions:
1. Drag this wammocheck bookmarklet to your bookmark bar.
2. Open up a Walmart Ammo page.
3. If the 'Find In Store' link is hidden, click bookmarklet to reveal it.
